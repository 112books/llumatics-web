**Bring Arezzo** — said the workshop announcement — and come learn to load, focus and shoot our old large format **Woody** camera.

The idea: we combine the experience of operating a large format camera with portrait technique. You arrive as a photographer and leave having also been a model. Or the other way round.

Best of all: **we developed the portraits immediately** in the shop's darkroom. All the magic of analogue photography, live.

Limited places — and they filled up fast.
