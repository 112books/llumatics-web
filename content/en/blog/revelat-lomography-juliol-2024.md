Another black-and-white developing workshop at **Cameras & Films** (formerly Lomography Embassy Barcelona).

Learning to develop a roll of film is one of those things that, once you've done it, you can't stop. Loading the film into the tank in the dark, measuring the chemicals, checking the temperature, agitating, waiting. And when you open the tank and see there's an image... there's nothing quite like it.
