Llumàtics structures its workshops around analogue photography understood as a complete process: from the initial exposure to the final print, covering both traditional techniques and experimental practices.

Workshops are organised around **five thematic areas** that allow students to build a coherent learning path, avoiding the fragmentation common in this type of offer.

All workshops at Llumàtics are private, with a **maximum of four students** per session. This reduced format allows close, individual support adapted to each participant's pace and interests, and guarantees a real hands-on experience with the darkroom and equipment.

Llumàtics also runs an **external workshops programme**, either on commission or through ongoing collaborations with art centres, photography schools and secondary schools. Notable is the relationship with [Cameras & Films](https://www.camerasandfilms.com) (formerly the Lomography Embassy Barcelona), where large format portrait courses are taught — using a late 19th-century camera and working directly on photosensitive paper —, alongside black and white film developing courses and experimental developing workshops.
