Llumàtics is at **Nau Bostik**, Carrer Ferran Turné 1-11, in the La Sagrera neighbourhood of Barcelona. A converted industrial space shared by artists, workshops and cultural projects.

The Llumàtics spaces are not open to the public on a drop-in basis. All visits are [by appointment](contacte) and workshops always run in groups of maximum four people.

The darkroom is the heart of Llumàtics. This isn't a beginner's lab: it's a fully equipped space for serious work, from the first session through to the final print.

**Enlargers:**
We have enlargers for **35mm**, **medium format** and **large format**. The large-format enlarger handles 4×5" sheet film directly onto paper. The medium-format enlargers accept all 120 formats (6×4.5, 6×6, 6×7, 6×9).

**Chemistry:**
Everything you need to develop and print in B&W: developers (Kodak D-76, Ilford ID-11, Rodinal), stop bath, fixers, washing aid and wetting agents. Chemistry is replenished regularly and temperature is controlled with a precision thermometer.

**Darkroom equipment:**
- Trays for 35mm, medium format and large format
- Fridge for films and chemical solutions
- Negative drying cabinet
- Glazing machine for RC paper prints
- Grain magnifier and negative viewing light
- Developing tanks in various sizes (Paterson, Jobo, tray)
- pH meters and lab thermometers

**Safety:**
The darkroom has forced ventilation, a chemical vapour extractor and a waste management protocol. Photographic chemicals are handled as special waste.

The Llumàtics studio is a working space, not a production set. Small, functional, equipped for portrait and product photography.

**Available equipment:**
- Studio flash heads with radio and sync-cable triggers
- Paper backdrops in various colours (white, black)
- Reflectors, diffusers and basic light modifiers
- Tripods and grip accessories
- Flash light meters

The studio is used in the **Analogue portrait**, **6×6 portrait** and **Photography tutorial** sessions. It is not available for separate hire.

## Scanning and editing station {#escaneig}
![Llumàtics scanning station](/images/espais/scan-zone.jpeg)

A functional scanning space, built for precision work with negatives and positives in all formats.

**Available scanners:**
- Epson Perfection V850 Pro film scanner (negatives up to 4×5", slides, paper)
- A4 flatbed scanner for prints and photographic documents

**Digital editing:**
- Colour-calibrated workstation (sRGB/Adobe RGB)
- Software: Lightroom Classic, Capture One, Photoshop
- ICC profiles for major paper and film types

The scanning station is used in **Scanning and digitalisation** workshops and **Photography tutorial** sessions.

## The library {#biblioteca}
![Llumàtics library](/images/espais/biblioteca.jpg)

A small but carefully chosen library. No decoration: every book here has been read or will be read.

**Main areas:**

*Photographic technique:* developing manuals (The Film Developing Cookbook, The Darkroom Cookbook, Ilford and Kodak guides), large-format technique books, B&W chemistry guides.

*Documentary and street photography:* Alex Coghe, Garry Winogrand, Josef Koudelka, Vivian Maier, Fan Ho, Doisneau, Cartier-Bresson, Castro Prieto, Rubén Morales.

*Alternative processes:* literature on cyanotype, calotype, gum bichromate and other nineteenth-century processes.

*Photobooks:* a collection of editorial photobooks, mainly from 112books and other independent European publishers.

The library is available to students during workshops. It is not a public reference library.
